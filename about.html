About me

Video games have been a large part of my life ever since I got a Gameboy Advance as a kid. Curiosity about what goes on behind the scenes and the desire to be a part of creating experiences is what has brought me into game development.

Graduated from The Game Assembly in Stockholm in April 2022 after finishing a 7 month internship at Zenz VR. Recently released a game, Wings 1941 [Meta Quest 2] while working at Ztar Games (formerly Zenz VR). Both during my time at The Game Assembly and at Zenz VR I've been a generalist programmer. I have knowledge of C++ and C#, experience with development in Unity and custom engines as well as working in small cross-disciplinary teams.
