About me

Video games have been a large part of my life ever since I got a Gameboy Advance as a kid. Curiosty over what goes into developing a game and the desire to be part of a team creating these experiences is what has taken into game development.

Recent graduate from The Game Assembly in Stockholm, just finished a 7 month internship at Zenz VR. Both during my time at The Game Assembly and at Zenz VR I've been a generalist programmer. I have knowledge of C++ and C#, experience with development in Unity and custom engines as well as working in small cross-disciplinary teams.
